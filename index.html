About Me

Meet Your Instructor

I am a certified yoga instructor and personal fitness coach passionate about helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals through mindful movement and structured training programs.

Over the past several years, I have worked with individuals from different backgrounds, including beginners starting their wellness journey, professionals managing stress, and fitness enthusiasts looking to improve flexibility and strength. My training philosophy focuses on building sustainable habits that support long-term physical and mental well-being.

Teaching Philosophy

My goal is to create a supportive environment where individuals can develop confidence in their bodies and gradually improve their strength, flexibility, and mental balance through consistent, mindful practice.

Pricing FAQs

Can I cancel my package?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly subscription at any time with at least 7 days' notice before the next billing cycle. We don't believe in long-term lock-in contracts.

Are sessions online or in-person?

Most our sessions are conducted via high-quality video calls, allowing you to practice from the comfort of your home. Selected in-person sessions are available for Premium clients in Mumbai.

What if I miss a session?

We offer a 24-hour rescheduling policy. As long as you notify us at least a day in advance, we'll find another time for your session within the same month.
